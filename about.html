About Gender Equality

Gender equality is the state in which people of all genders have equal rights, responsibilities, and opportunities. It promotes fairness in education, employment, political participation, and access to healthcare.

Despite progress, gender disparities still exist in many societies, affecting economic development, health outcomes, and social well-being.

Achieving gender equality benefits everyone by creating a more just and inclusive world.
