Gender Equality Statistics

Here are some important facts to understand the current scenario:

  • Women hold only about 28% of managerial positions globally.
  • Gender pay gap remains significant: women earn approximately 83% of what men earn.
  • Only 25% of parliamentary seats worldwide are held by women.
  • Gender-based violence affects billions each year.
  • Access to education for girls is still limited in some regions.
